Our Story

Love & Faith is all about offering affordable Christian apparel. We strive to create quality clothing with sayings/scriptures that remind us of God's love. We believe it is important to show your faith and be not afraid of what others may say or think. Wrap yourself in confidence and faith with our quality and comfy clothing. We also strive to help out others in our community by promoting 3 different Christian charities for our customers to support each quarter.
