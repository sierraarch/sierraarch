Value Proposition

Value Proposition: Sustainable Hair accessories for a good cause

Vision

C.HAIR.i.TEE believes that you don't need to sacrifice your style, quality, or comfort to live sustainably. C.HAIR.i.TEE uses recycled t-shirts to create a variety of hair accessories and lessen textile waste. Every quarter they donate all of their profits to different charities. C.HAIR.i.TEE wants people to accessorize with a good cause behind it.

3 Strategies to Support Vision

  • Use fabric scraps from donations from customers and companies
  • Use compostable packaging to ship orders
  • Help other companies succeed with their profits
