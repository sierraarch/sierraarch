Travel/Immigration

The government has banned anyone from China, Iran, Europe, United Kingdom, Republic of Ireland and Brazil from entering the U.S.

People/Businesses

Government enacted the CARES Act to help American workers, families, businesses jobs.

This act provides fast and direct economic assistance to those in need or affected for COVID.

Eligible Americans received a check for $1200 per person from the government as part of this act.

Education

Many schools moved online and interest on student loans has been cut until Dec 31,2020.

Housing

Governor Polis made an executive order banning evictions from about Mar-May 2020.

Since September this has now changed, and they can now take people to court within 30 days notice.
